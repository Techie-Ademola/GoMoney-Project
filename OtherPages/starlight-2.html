About the Campaign

Tough times don't last, but tough people do! We understand that everyone has wishes or those things on your wishlist you will love to acquire. You have worked hard and you deserve to get that which you wish for.

This is why we, your friends at gomoney, have put together the ‘What do you deserve’ challenge to enable our new and existing customers to make a wish and we will grant these wishes. Yes, wishes, from multiple entries.

But to qualify, you have to meet certain terms and conditions:

  • You must have an active gomoney account
  • Be sure you are following the gomoney page on Facebook, Instagram and Twitter
  • You must be available to claim your wish if selected
  • All information must be correct for easy identification
  • You must be 18 years and above with proof of identification

Share the challenge with your friends and family to have a higher chance of winning directly or indirectly.

Tip: Owning a physical card will be an advantage.

Disqualification:
  • Carefully write down what you deserve in the text box as multiple and unclear entries could lead to disqualification
  • Please insert the right contact details and ensure you monitor your means of contact as failure to establish a connection after several attempts could lead to disqualification
